~ Social Studies Lab ~

NPS provides a very well-equipped social science lab consisting of numerous charts, still and working models. Students of different grades are taken to the lab regularly. Social science quizzes, exhibitions and group discussions are held at regular intervals, which enable the students to learn while doing. Social science lab provides a platform for students to share their presentations.Social Science Lab is a distinguishing feature of our school where children come and learn practically which helps them to think out of the box.
